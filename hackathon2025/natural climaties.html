Natural Calamity Preparedness Guide

Follow these essential steps before, during, and after any natural calamity to stay safe.

  • 1. Stay informed: Always follow IMD alerts, government warnings, radio updates, and mobile emergency notifications.

  • 2. Prepare an emergency kit: Include water, food, torch, power bank, medicines, first-aid kit, and important documents.

  • 3. Evacuation plan: Know safe assembly points, nearby shelters, and evacuation routes in your locality.

  • 4. During the disaster: Stay calm, move to a safe place, follow official instructions, and avoid risky areas like floodwaters and unstable buildings.

  • 5. After the disaster: Check for injuries, avoid damaged structures, boil drinking water, and stay aware of aftershocks or secondary hazards.

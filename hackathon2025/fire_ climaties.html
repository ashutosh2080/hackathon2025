Fire Emergency Action Guide

Follow these steps immediately during a fire to protect yourself and others.

  • 1. Alert everyone: Warn others and call the Fire Brigade (101) immediately.

  • 2. Use fire extinguisher: If the fire is small and safe, aim at the base of the flames when using an extinguisher.

  • 3. Evacuate immediately: Leave the building quickly and stay low to avoid smoke inhalation.

  • 4. Close doors behind you: This helps slow the spread of fire and smoke.

  • 5. Do not re-enter: Never go back inside a burning building. Wait for firefighters.

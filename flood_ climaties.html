Flood Safety Checklist

Use this essential checklist to stay safe and prepared before and during floods.

  • Identify high ground:
    Know safe elevated areas and emergency evacuation routes in your locality.

  • Prepare a 48-hour Go-Bag:
    Water, non-perishable food, medicines, first-aid kit, and essential documents.

  • Store documents safely:
    Keep important documents sealed in waterproof bags.

  • Move valuables to higher ground:
    Keep electronics, cash, jewelry, and essentials on upper shelves or top floors.

  • Turn off electricity & gas:
    If advised by authorities, shut off power and gas supply for safety.

  • Avoid floodwaters:
    Never walk or drive through moving water. Even shallow water can be deadly.

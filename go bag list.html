Ultimate Emergency Go-Bag Checklist

A 48-hour survival kit for floods, earthquakes, cyclones, fires, and emergency evacuations.

  • Water (3 liters per person per day)
  • Non-perishable food (minimum 3 days)
  • Flashlight + extra batteries
  • First-aid kit & essential medicines
  • Waterproof pouch with ID & documents
  • Power bank + charging cables
  • Warm clothing, sturdy shoes & emergency cash
